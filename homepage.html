The Challenge We're Solving

Small-scale fishers often lack transparent, technology-driven ways to prove that their catch is legal, traceable, and sustainable. Without quick, verifiable data on catch origin and fisher credentials, restaurants and buyers struggle to make responsible purchasing decisions. This lack of transparency allows illegal, unreported, and unregulated (IUU) fishing to thrive —an industry that steals millions of tons of fish from the ocean each year, causes tens of billions of dollars in economic losses, and threatens global food security for nearly one billion people who rely on fish as their primary source of protein.
